Topic-description An in-depth look into cyber crime that occurs in the healthcare sector; giving insight into the types of attacks, motives behind said attacks, and the groups and individuals responsible. We have chosen to investigate cybercrime in the healthcare sector due to the relevance and implications it poses as a societal threat.
